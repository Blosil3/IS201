About Me

I am born and raised in Utah, but after I graduated from High School I moved on my own to New York City. This was a pivitol moment in my life. I was able to live in one of the greatest cities in the world and developed self reliance, grit, and helped expand my horizion on the possibilities of life.

I was diagnosed with leukemia the day after my wedding in the summer of 2017. This trial through me, and my wife, for a loop. Seven years later, this experience has taught me the ability to overcome adversity and resiliance.

I have a wide range of hobbies ranging from cooking and baking to Crossfit. I'm obsessed with skiing and some of my favorite memories are skipping school for power days (I've got an incredible mom). I'm an amatuer music producer with a passion for house music. If I'm not doing any of these things, I'm either reading random news articles, wikipedia pages, or spending time with my wife and daughter.
